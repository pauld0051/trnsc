Healthy Tips to Manage Diabetes

Managing type 2 diabetes is a journey, but with the right tools, anyone can take control of their health. Here are a few tips to keep your blood sugar stable and your body healthy.

1. Choose Whole Grains

Whole grains, such as brown rice, oats, and quinoa, are rich in fiber and nutrients that help manage blood sugar levels. However, itâ€™s important to watch your portion sizes. Aim to fill no more than a quarter of your plate with whole grains, and balance your meal with plenty of vegetables and lean proteins.

2. Stay Active

Regular exercise helps improve insulin sensitivity and manage blood sugar levels. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate activity, like walking or swimming, most days of the week.

3. Eat Healthy Fats

Healthy fats, such as those found in avocados, nuts, and olive oil, can help reduce insulin resistance and keep your blood sugar levels more stable. Include these in your diet in moderation.

4. Avoid Sugary Drinks

Sugary beverages can cause blood sugar spikes. Instead, opt for water, unsweetened teas, or sparkling water with a splash of lemon or lime.

5. Monitor Your Blood Sugar Regularly

Regular monitoring helps you understand how different foods and activities affect your blood sugar. Consult with your healthcare provider to determine the best times to check your levels.
